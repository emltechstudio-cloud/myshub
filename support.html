Frequently Asked Questions
How do I customize my shop?
Go to the Edit Shop page from your dashboard. You can change your shop name, description, add links, and connect your social media accounts.
Can I use a custom domain?
Custom domains are available on the Pro and Business plans. Upgrade your plan to connect your own domain.
How do I track my shop visits?
Your dashboard shows total visits and today's visits. For detailed analytics including click tracking and visitor trends, visit the Analytics page.
How do I delete my account?
To delete your account permanently, please contact our support team using the form below. Account deletion cannot be undone.
